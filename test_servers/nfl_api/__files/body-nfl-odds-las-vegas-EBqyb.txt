<h2>How to read NFL Las Vegas Odds</h2>

<p>The point-spread was developed to provide a balance for both teams involved in a contest to entice bettors to potentially back the weaker team and receive points. The two squads in a game are listed with a title, either a favorite or an underdog. The favorite is usually the perceived better team in the game, as backing them means giving up several points.</p> 

<p>The favorite is always listed with a <strong>minus (-)</strong> sign before the point-spread while the underdog is labeled with a <strong>plus (+)</strong> label.</p> 

<h3>Ex. Favorite -10, Underdog +10</h3>

<p>On the VegasInsider.com odds page, there is another number associated with the favorite and its listed as <strong>-10</strong>. This number is simply defined as <strong>vig</strong> or what many in the sports betting industry call <strong>vigorish</strong>. Another common term is called <strong>juice</strong> and its technically the price the bettor has to pay on a straight wager.</p> 

<h3>Ex. Bet $110 to win $100 (10% juice)<br>
Ex. Bet $100 to win $91.91 (10% juice)
</h3>

<p>It's not uncommon to see other values posted other than -10. Examples seen on the NFL Vegas Odds pages could include <strong>-08</strong>, <strong>-12</strong>, <strong>-15</strong> and <strong>-20</strong>. The -10 price is the most common value in the industry while many books offer reduced 'juice odds' and that would fall into the -08 category.</p>

<p>The <strong>lower-juice sportsbooks</strong> are normally found outside of the state Nevada. If you are in a state where sports betting is legal, please check out our <a class="newstext" href="https://www.vegasinsider.com/sportsbook-directory/">online sportsbook directory</a> to find the best and most secure places to make NFL bets.</p>

<p>Another number thats posted on the NFL Las Vegas is the <strong>total</strong> or <strong>over/under</strong> for the specific matchup. If the favorite is designated as the home team, then the total will be listed above and vice versa if the visitors are favorites.</p> 

<p>All of the above numbers are listed next to the teams, and before each matchup is a <strong>Rotation</strong> number. The NFL Las Vegas Odds are listed in order of rotation and those numbers are generated and produced by the sportsbooks. Above each matchup and rotation is the <strong>Time</strong> of the game, which is subject to change. All game times are Eastern Standard Time.
